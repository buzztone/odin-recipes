Squash Casserole

Description

When summer squash season rolls around and you need a way to use up your yellow squash bounty, what better dish to make than a creamy, cheesy, and buttery squash casserole?

The Southern classic combines fresh squash and onion with a creamy sauce and a crunchy cracker-Cheddar cheese topping.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups sliced yellow squash
  • 1 cup shredded Cheddar cheese
  • 2 large eggs, beaten
  • Â¾ cup milk

Steps

  1. Place squash and onion in a large skillet over medium heat; pour in water. Cover, and cook until squash is tender, about 5 minutes. Drain well, and place in a large bowl.
  2. Mix eggs and milk together in a small bowl, then add to squash mixture.
  3. Stir in 1/4 cup melted butter, and season with salt and pepper. Spread into the prepared baking dish.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned, about 25 minutes.
